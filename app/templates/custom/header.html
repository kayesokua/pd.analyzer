About

Pole dancing is a performance art that combines dance and acrobatics around a vertical pole. It has ancient origins, but gained acceptance as a legitimate form of dance and fitness in the late 20th century. This capstone project aims to leverage the power of data science to enhance pole dancing by developing a Dance Summarization Model, specifically for solo pole dancing performances. The model will provide feedback that could contain a summary of poses detected, movement quality, describe the effort, and other insights that could be explored.
